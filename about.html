About Chay


Chay is an experienced performer, entertaining audiences all over the UK and internationally for over 10 years, starting with his fascination with magic as child, Chay quickly built a reputation as a skilled performer in the art of magic, specialising in magic with cards, coins and everyday objects. As his career progressed, he developed a unique ability to apply his skill to mentalism. Using a variety of techniques, psychology, hypnosis-suggestion, magic, instinct and showmanship, Chay has built a mind reading act that will amaze, and entertain all. Blending these two fields together makes a unique, fun experience everyone can enjoy!



About the Performances


Close up / Walk around

Up close and personal, see the magic up close and in your own hands! Chay will mingle with your guests, performing 10 -15minutes with small groups of 2 - 8 people, Chay will move around the event giving these small performances keep guests entertained. Want to fill a gap between courses at a wedding? waiting for the dj to set up? Then this is the perfect light, sideline entertainment to make sure everyones having a good time. This performance is best suited for weddings, birthday parties, fairs, discos. This performance is bookable for a minium of 1 hour up to a max of 3 hours.

Parlour

A mix between close up and stage, this is an intimate performance for groups of 10 people up to 30. Like a stage show but smaller, Imagine a stage show without the stage. This performance is perfect for a main act in a small event. No stage required, just a small area for the performer and an area for the audience to watch and join in. This act can be booked for a minium of 30 minutes up to an hour . This performance is best suited as main rather than sideline entertainment and is good for birthday parties, small gatherings, small venues.

Stage Show

This is Chay main show, available in two styles the full show (50 minutes) or half show (20 minutes). The show is best suited to venues with more than 30 guests. Want to book an exciting, entertaining, interactive show with a difference! Then this is for you! Whether you need to fill a slot in a show or need a main act this magical act will amaze your audiences!
